How To Read It

Use the chart like an operator

Look for negative spikes

Sudden negative surges usually map to a release issue, policy change, or support breakdown.

Watch total volume too

A big sentiment move with low volume may be noise. The dashed total line helps separate signal from isolated comments.

Compare windows after launches

The period comparison is most useful before and after releases, campaigns, migrations, and backlog cleanup cycles.
